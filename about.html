Who We Are

Velonixs Technology was founded with a vision to bridge the gap between business challenges and technology solutions. In today's rapidly evolving digital landscape, organizations require technology partners who understand not only software development but also business processes, scalability, security, and long-term maintainability.

Our expertise spans enterprise software development, cloud computing, application modernization, DevOps implementation, and dedicated engineering services. We focus on building long-term partnerships and helping our clients achieve sustainable business growth through technology innovation.

Headquartered in India | Serving Clients Globally
